Featuring prominent healthcare leaders Zayna Khayat from SE Health and Chris Vigna from Pyramid Healthcare

With the world returning to pre-pandemic normalcy, will healthcare organizations continue to embrace telehealth as they did during the pandemic?

 

Join OnCall Health’s webinar on August 10, 2021, along with panelists Zayna Khayat, SE Health, and Chris Vigna, Pyramid Healthcare Inc., as we answer critical questions about telehealth and its pivotal role in the future of healthcare. We’ll address important questions– What does it mean to transform homes into healthcare centers? How do we incentivize mental healthcare leaders to continue to 'put the patient first' with telemedicine?  They’ll also share tactics for rethinking the healthcare experience, like how remote care and in-person care can work together to create high-impact patient experiences.

What we'll cover:

  • How mental healthcare organizations can adapt their healthcare operations for a post-pandemic world 
  • Policy and regulatory changes required to embrace a truly hybrid care model
  • How to design an omni-channel care experience for providers and patients
